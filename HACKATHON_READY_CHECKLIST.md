## 🎬 7-Minute Demo Script

### Introduction (30 seconds)
"S.E.A.D. is an enterprise document approval system that replaces scattered email threads and local folders with a single, role-secured repository. We support 9-level approval workflows, 5 business system integrations, and automated compliance."

### Act 1: The Problem (30 seconds)
"In most organizations, documents are scattered across email, shared drives, and multiple systems. Approvals get stuck. Nobody knows where a document is in its lifecycle. Manual follow-ups waste time."

### Act 2: Centralized Management (1 minute)
1. Show dashboard with request overview
2. Create new request with attachments
3. Show full-text search
4. Show version history
5. "Everything in one place, fully searchable, with complete history."

### Act 3: Smart Workflow (1.5 minutes)
1. Show 9-level approval workflow diagram
2. Process a request (approve with notes)
3. Show automated email notification
4. Raise a query for clarification
5. Show query response flow
6. "The system routes to the right approver, sends reminders, and handles clarifications automatically."

### Act 4: Business Integrations (2 minutes)
1. Show Settings → Gmail Connected
2. Open a request
3. Click "Link to ERP/CRM/HR"
4. Link to Odoo invoice
5. Link to CRM contact
6. Link to HRM employee
7. Show all links displayed
8. Click "View" to open in external system
9. "We integrate with 5 major systems. No manual data entry. No duplicate records."

### Act 5: Compliance & Analytics (1 minute)
1. Show SLA analytics dashboard
2. Show compliance dashboard
3. Show retention policies
4. Show GDPR data export
5. "Automated compliance, retention policies, and audit trails for regulations."

### Conclusion (30 seconds)
"S.E.A.D. delivers faster turnaround, fewer lost files, and clear visibility. Organizations gain productivity and compliance. Employees get a single, reliable place to manage documents with automated workflows keeping everything moving."
